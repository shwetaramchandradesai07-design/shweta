Incredible India Tourism

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is an ivory-white marble mausoleum commissioned in 1632 by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan for his wife Mumtaz Mahal.Famous for the Taj Mahal, one of the seven wonders.

  • Best time:October to March

Kerla Backwaters

Known for serene backwaters and houseboats.The Kerala Backwaters are a 900–1500 km network of brackish lagoons, lakes, canals, and rivers running parallel to the Arabian Sea coast, primarily in southern India.Famous for "houseboat" cruises.

  • Best time:June to March

Haridwar

Haridwar is not only a city but is an experience. The city in Uttarakhand is also known as the gateway to the Himalayas . It is an important destination for Hindus across the globe. Lakhs of visitors every year come to Haridwar in search of spiritual enlightenment and peace.

  • Best time:November to April

Munnar

Famous for the tea estates, greenery, winding roads, blanket of mist, and viewpoints, Munnar is a hill station in Kerala, located in the Idukki district. Lying in the Western Ghats at 1600 metres, it is one of the most sought after and visited travel destinations globally.

  • Best Time: September to May

Ooty

Ooty, the "Queen of Hill Stations" in Tamil Nadu, offers breathtaking attractions including the scenic Nilgiri Mountain Railway, pristine Pykara Lake/Falls, sprawling Botanical Gardens, and panoramic Doddabetta Peak. Key sights include Avalanche Lake, Rose Garden, Tea Museum,Coonoor viewpoints.

  • Best time:March to June

Ellora Cave

An UNESCO World Heritage Site, Ellora Caves are the most visited places of heritage in Maharashtra, and among the must include places in Maharashtra tour packages. Situated about 28 km from Aurangabad, 253 km from Pune, and 344 km from Mumbai.

  • Best time:january to March

Goa

​Situated in the Northern Goa area of Goa, Old Goa alludes to the notable town that served as the seat of the Portuguese in India till the eighteenth century. The city is likewise a UNESCO World Heritage Site because of its one of a kind Baroque style engineering and different houses of worship.

  • Best time:November to March

Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the most prominent tourist places in India. The city attracts millions of domestic and international tourists. Varanasi is one of the oldest cities in the world, which makes it rich with cultural heritage. The city is home to ghats, temples, and museums.

  • Best time:October to March

Gangtok

Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, is a vibrant, modern hill station nestled in the Eastern Himalayas at 1,437 meters. Known for its stunning views of Mount Kanchenjunga and bustling MG Marg, it serves as a top travel destination offering a blend of Buddhist culture, monasteries (Rumtek, Enchey), and scenic high-altitude lakes like Tsomgo Lake.

  • Best time:Throughout the year
